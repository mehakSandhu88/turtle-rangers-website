CODE OF CONDUCT
1. Respect each other's opinions and ideas
Treat all team members respectfully and professionally, regardless of their roles or background.
CODE OF CONDUCT
2. Communicate openly and consistently
Encourage team members to communicate openly and respectfully with one another. Ensure that everyone has a chance to share their ideas and opinions without fear of judgment or criticism.
CODE OF CONDUCT
3. Be open to constructive criticism and feedback
Encourage open and honest feedback from all team members on a regular basis. Be receptive to feedback from others, and use it to learn and grow as a team.
CODE OF CONDUCT
4. Have a flexible approach
Be flexible and adaptable in the face of unexpected challenges or changes to the project. Stay open-minded and willing to try new approaches or solutions.
CODE OF CONDUCT
5. Resolve Conflict Responsibly
1. Establish a process for resolving conflicts in a respectful and constructive manner

2. Encourage open dialogue and provide a safe space for discussing concerns

3. Seek mutually agreeable solutions and focus on the best interests of the team and the project.
CODE OF CONDUCT
6. Always Celebrate successes
Recognize and celebrate the achievements of individuals and the team as a whole. This can help boost morale and promote a positive attitude towards work.
CODE OF CONDUCT
7. Hold each other accountable
Make sure that everyone is accountable for their tasks and responsibilities. If someone is falling behind, it is important to address the issue and find a solution together as a team.
